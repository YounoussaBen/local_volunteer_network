As a volunteer, you are responsible for providing accurate information, maintaining the confidentiality of your account, and respecting the organizations you work with.

3. Code of Conduct

We expect all volunteers to conduct themselves professionally and respectfully when engaging with organizations and fellow volunteers.

When you apply for and are accepted to volunteer opportunities, you are making a commitment to fulfill those obligations to the best of your ability.

Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, location, skills, and interests to help match you with appropriate volunteer opportunities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We share your profile information with organizations when you apply for their volunteer opportunities. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings.
