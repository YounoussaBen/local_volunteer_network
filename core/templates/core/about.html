Our Story

How It All Started

Local Volunteer Network was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a centralized platform that connects willing volunteers with meaningful opportunities in Accra.

We noticed that despite the strong spirit of volunteerism in our community, there was no efficient way for volunteers to find opportunities that matched their skills and interests. Similarly, organizations struggled to find dedicated volunteers for their initiatives.

Our founders, a group of tech enthusiasts and social impact advocates, decided to build a solution that would bridge this gap and make volunteering more accessible to everyone in Accra.

Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To facilitate meaningful connections between volunteers and organizations in Accra, creating a more engaged and supportive community through the power of volunteerism.

We aim to make volunteering accessible to everyone, regardless of their background, by providing a user-friendly platform that matches volunteers with opportunities that align with their skills, interests, and availability.

We believe that by connecting people with causes they care about, we can create a positive impact that ripples throughout our community.

Our Vision

We envision a future where volunteering is an integral part of life in Accra, where every citizen is empowered to contribute their skills and time to causes they care about.

Our platform aims to be the go-to resource for volunteer engagement, fostering a culture of giving back and community support throughout Ghana.

By 2026, we aim to have connected over 10,000 volunteers with organizations, creating a network of active citizens who are committed to making Accra a better place for all.
