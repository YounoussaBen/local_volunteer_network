Questions for Volunteers

Our platform uses a matching algorithm to recommend opportunities based on your profile. When you register, you'll be asked to provide information about your skills, interests, and availability.

You can also use the search and filter functions to browse all available opportunities and narrow down by location, skills required, cause areas, and more.

After you submit an application, the organization will review your profile and application message. You'll receive a notification when they update your application status (accepted, declined, or pending).

If accepted, you'll be able to communicate with the organization through our platform to coordinate details. If declined, you can check other opportunities that might be a better fit.

Time commitments vary depending on the opportunity. Some may be one-time events that last a few hours, while others might require regular commitment over weeks or months.

Each opportunity listing includes details about the time commitment required, helping you find opportunities that fit your schedule. You can also filter opportunities based on duration and frequency.

Yes! After completing a volunteer opportunity, you'll be prompted to provide feedback about your experience. This helps organizations improve and helps other volunteers make informed decisions.

Organizations can also provide feedback about volunteers, which can help build your volunteer profile and reputation on the platform.

Your volunteer dashboard automatically tracks all your accepted and completed volunteer opportunities. For each completed opportunity, you'll see the estimated hours based on the opportunity details.

In the future, we plan to add a more detailed hour-tracking feature where both volunteers and organizations can log specific hours for better accuracy.

Questions for Organizations

After registering and completing your organization profile, you can post volunteer opportunities by clicking on the "Create Opportunity" button in your dashboard.

You'll need to provide details such as title, description, location, date/time, required skills, and the number of volunteers needed. The more detailed your listing, the better matches you'll find!

No, there is no limit to the number of opportunities you can post. You can create as many opportunities as you need to support your organization's mission.

We encourage organizations to keep their listings current by marking opportunities as "filled" or "completed" when appropriate.

When a volunteer applies for your opportunity, you'll receive a notification. You can review all applications in the "Applications" section of your dashboard.

For each application, you can view the volunteer's profile, including their skills, interests, and past volunteer experience. You can then accept or decline the application with an optional message to the volunteer.

While our platform allows volunteers to list their skills, we do not verify these skills directly. However, you can review a volunteer's profile, past volunteer history, and any ratings or feedback they've received from other organizations.

We recommend conducting your own screening process for opportunities that require specific certifications or expertise. You can also use the messaging feature to ask volunteers additional questions before accepting their applications.

To get verified, please send us an email at verification@localvolunteer.net with the following information:

  • Your organization's official registration documents
  • A brief description of your organization's mission and activities
  • Contact information for verification

Our team will review your submission and typically complete the verification process within 5-7 business days.

Technical Questions

Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use encryption to protect your personal information and follow industry best practices for data security.

We only share your information with organizations when you apply for their volunteer opportunities. You can review our privacy policy for more details on how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Our platform is designed to work on all modern devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.

The platform is fully responsive, meaning it will adjust to fit your screen size regardless of the device you're using.

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please check your spam folder. If you still need assistance, contact our support team at support@localvolunteer.net.

To update your profile, log in to your account and click on your profile picture or username in the top-right corner. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu.

On your profile page, click the "Edit Profile" button to update your personal information, skills, interests, and availability. Don't forget to save your changes when you're done.

If you wish to delete your account, please go to your profile settings and select the "Account" tab. At the bottom of the page, you'll find an option to "Delete Account."

Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data from our platform, including your profile, application history, and messages. If you're just taking a break, consider deactivating your account instead, which can be reactivated later.
